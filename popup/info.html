About Reply Better AI

Reply Better AI is a browser extension that helps you improve your messages professionally using free AI models through OpenRouter.

Version: 1.0

License: MIT

Features

  • Professional text improvement using AI
  • Multiple message types: Professional, Friendly, Customer Service, Concise
  • Custom prompt creation for personalized improvements
  • Text snippets feature for quick text expansion
  • Uses free AI models only - no subscription needed
